Scavengers

Scavengers as the name suggests, Sc-Scientist Avengers is a instagram based comic series launched by the students of NIT Agartala, till now the comic is been released locally for the students of NIT and is not been promoted on a vast media.With time team is looking forward to move ahead with the idea of releasing the comic for everyone who is keen to look after the textbook scientists in a different look. Scavengers is entirely a work of fiction and does not promote any kind of false assumption, hatred, violence etc.
